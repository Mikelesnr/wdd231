Discover Harare

Mkwisi Woodlands

Mkwisi Woodlands, Harare

Explore the serene beauty of Harare's Mkwisi Woodlands, perfect for picnics and nature walks.

Mkwisi Woodlands

Mkwisi Woodlands offers a peaceful retreat within Harare. With lush greenery and scenic trails, it's a haven for families, picnics, and adventurers alike.

Mkwisi Woodlands offers visitors an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. This serene location is perfect for nature walks, picnics, and exploring lush greenery. Wildlife spotting is also popular, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts and photographers alike.

The woodlands frequently host guided tours and educational walks for schools and families. It's also a favored spot for yoga sessions, bird-watching, and seasonal festivals celebrating Harare's natural heritage.

Cleveland Dam

Cleveland Dam, Harare

A scenic reservoir offering tranquil views and recreational activities.

Cleveland Dam

Cleveland Dam provides breathtaking views, a variety of bird species, and outdoor activities and serene boating experiences.

It is a tranquil reservoir ideal for a day of relaxation. Visitors can enjoy fishing, hiking along scenic trails, or simply admiring the breathtaking water views and surrounding landscapes.

The area is a popular destination for group picnics and outdoor games. Additionally, the dam supports bird-watching enthusiasts, with various species frequenting its shores throughout the year.

Africa Unity Square

Nelson Mandela Avenue, Harare

A historic park in the heart of Harare, known for its fountains and flower vendors.

Africa Unity Square

This central square is a vibrant gathering spot adorned with fountains and gardens. It's a cultural hub celebrating Harare’s heritage and community spirit.

Africa Unity Square serves as a historic landmark and a vibrant social hub in the heart of Harare. Its manicured gardens, water fountains, and pathways make it an ideal location for peaceful strolls and family outings.

The square hosts cultural events, art exhibitions, and flower markets regularly. It's also a favorite spot for photographers capturing Harare's charm and local vendors selling unique crafts.

Parliament Building

Parliament Building, Harare

An iconic structure symbolizing Zimbabwe's governance and history.

Parliament Building

The Parliament Building is a significant landmark in Harare, showcasing colonial architecture and hosting important government functions.

It is not just a hub of governance but also a symbol of Zimbabwe's rich history. Visitors admire its grand design and learn about the country's political framework through guided tours.

The building occasionally hosts public events and educational programs aimed at increasing civic awareness. Its central location makes it easily accessible for those interested in history and government affairs.

Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe

Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe, Harare

The tallest building in Harare, showcasing modern architecture and economic significance.

Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe

This towering structure represents Harare’s economic resilience and houses vital financial institutions of national importance.

The Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe, the tallest building in Harare, showcases modern architectural brilliance. Visitors admire its imposing structure and its role as a cornerstone of the country's financial system.

The plaza around the bank often features business-oriented events, financial seminars, and community programs promoting economic literacy. It's a must-see for those interested in modern cityscapes and economic landmarks.

Cleveland Rifle Range

Cleveland Rifle Range, Harare

A popular spot for shooting sports enthusiasts and outdoor activities.

Cleveland Rifle Range

Cleveland Rifle Range hosts shooting practice and tournaments in a safe and controlled outdoor setting.

This is the go-to destination for shooting enthusiasts and professionals. It features state-of-the-art facilities for various shooting sports, including rifle and pistol competitions.

The range frequently hosts training sessions for beginners and experienced shooters alike. It's also a great place to connect with local shooting clubs and experience a unique recreational activity.

Sam Levy Village

Sam Levy Village, Harare

A vibrant shopping and entertainment hub in Harare's northern suburbs.

Sam Levy Village

This bustling hub combines high-end shopping, fine dining, and cultural entertainment, making it a favorite among locals and visitors.

Sam Levy Village is the ultimate destination for shopping and entertainment in Harare. Visitors enjoy high-end boutiques, local craft stores, and an array of restaurants serving international and traditional Zimbabwean cuisine.

The village also hosts live music, outdoor cinema nights, and seasonal festivals. It's a bustling community space that offers a vibrant atmosphere for both residents and tourists.

Harare Showgrounds

Harare Showgrounds, Harare

The venue for the annual Harare Agricultural Show and other major events.

Harare Showgrounds

The Harare Showgrounds host Zimbabwe’s largest agricultural show and other events.

This is a lively venue that hosts Harare's annual Agricultural Show, one of the country's most anticipated events. The show features exhibitions, livestock displays, and family-friendly activities.

Throughout the year, the grounds are used for trade fairs, concerts, and community gatherings. It’s a versatile space that reflects the vibrant agricultural and cultural traditions of Zimbabwe.
