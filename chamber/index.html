Harare Agricultural Show

The Harare Agricultural Show is Zimbabwe's largest annual event celebrating agriculture, innovation, and community. Join us for an exciting week filled with exhibitions, livestock displays, live performances, and networking opportunities. Discover the latest in agricultural advancements, enjoy local cuisine, and connect with farmers and industry leaders. Don't miss this vibrant showcase of Zimbabwe's agricultural heritage and future!
