Digijobs Site Plan

A comprehensive guide for the design and development of the Digijobs platform.

Site Name

Digijobs - The name was chosen to reflect the site's purpose of bridging the gap between job seekers and employers using digital solutions.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Digijobs is to connect job seekers with potential employers. The site will facilitate job searches, application submissions, and employer postings in an intuitive, user-friendly interface.

Scenarios

  • For Job Seekers: "How can I find job opportunities in my field?"
  • For Employers: "How do I post a job listing and manage applicants?"

Color Scheme

The site will use the following color palette:

  • #86b995 (Green-Tone): For headers, links, and main accents to convey calm professionalism.
  • #eedfff (Soft Lavender): For call-to-action buttons and highlights to draw attention with elegance.
  • #857373 (Muted Brown): For sections and cards' backgrounds to create visual separation.
  • #291d1d (Dark Charcoal): For primary content areas to provide a rich, bold contrast.
  • #c4baba (White): For text to ensure readability and a subtle, warm appearance.

Typography

The site will use the following fonts:

  • Roboto: For headings, ensuring readability and a modern feel.
  • Merriweather: For body text, providing a clean and professional look.

Wireframe

Below is the wireframe for the Digijobs homepage:

  • Mobile View: A single-column layout with a navigation bar at the top, followed by featured job listings, and footer links.
  • Desktop View: A two-column layout with a navigation bar at the top, a featured jobs section on the left, and employer tools on the right.

Wireframe diagrams will be created as sketches for both views.
